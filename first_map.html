Florida Music Festivals Map

The map above marks four of Florida's popular music festivals. EDC Orlando and Ultra in Miami are two popular electronic music festivals. Tortuga Festival in Fort Lauderdale is a country music festival on the beach. Okeechobee Music & Arts Festival includes a range of music genres and is popular for camping. Florida is slowly introducing newer music festivals such as III Points Festival in Miami's Wynwood. With its amazing weather, hopefully the state gets more music festivals to match the likes of California and New York.
