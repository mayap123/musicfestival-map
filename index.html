USA Music Festivals Map

Perhaps you have heard of Coachella, the popular music festival in California's desert attended by many celebrities and influencers. But the United States hosts plenty of other music festivals as well. From hip-hop to country, these festivals offer something for fans of all different genres. The map above includes the locations for 20 popular music festivals that take place in the United States.
